# Product Requirements Document (PRD) - MASAE Delivery Tracker

## 1. Project Overview
**Project Name:** MASAE Delivery Tracker
**Version:** 2.0.0
**Status:** Active Development
**Objective:** A comprehensive logistics and delivery management system designed to track agricultural inputs (fertilizers) from production to final distribution points across multiple geographical regions.

## 2. Target Audience
- **Managers:** Oversee entire projects, monitor global stats, manage fleet, and validate expenses.
- **Operators (Cooperatives/GIE):** Manage local allocations, receive deliveries, and track progress.
- **Drivers:** Execute deliveries and provide real-time status updates.

## 3. Problem Statement
Agricultural distribution campaigns involve complex logistics: multiple regions, hundreds of trucks, and thousands of tons of fertilizer. Manual tracking via paper leads to:
- Lack of real-time visibility on delivery progress.
- Errors in tonnage reporting.
- Delays in generating "Bon de Livraison" (BL) and "Fin de Cession" (FC) documents.
- Difficulties in managing truck queues and driver assignments.

## 4. Goals & Success Metrics
- **Digitization:** 100% of deliveries recorded digitally.
- **Real-time Tracking:** Instant dashboard updates on tonnage delivered vs. targeted.
- **Automation:** Reduce document generation time from minutes to seconds.
- **Fleet Optimization:** Efficient FIFO (First-In-First-Out) management for truck loading.

## 5. Functional Requirements

### 5.1 User Authentication & Roles
- **Manager (Level 2):** Full access to all modules, including financial data and project settings.
- **Operator (Level 1):** Access to specific project allocations and delivery views.
- **Driver (Level 0):** View assigned tasks and delivery history.
- **Security:** Password-protected access with session management via AuthContext.

### 5.2 Fleet Management
- **Trucks:** Track plate numbers, trailer details, chassis numbers, and ownership (Internal vs. External).
- **Drivers:** Manage driver profiles, licenses, and contact info.
- **Assignment:** Dynamic linking of drivers to trucks.
- **Status Tracking:** Monitor availability of the fleet.

### 5.3 Project & Allocation Management
- **Geographical Hierarchy:** Support for Regions -> Departments -> Communes.
- **Allocations:** Define tonnage targets for specific operators within a project phase.
- **Visibility:** Toggle project visibility to focus on active campaigns.

### 5.4 Delivery Workflow (FIFO)
- **Queue Management:** Trucks enter a "Waiting" status and are processed in order.
- **Loading Interface:** Record tonnage loaded, assign to an allocation, and generate a unique BL number.
- **QR Code Integration:** Generate unique QR codes for each delivery for verification.

### 5.5 Reporting & Views
- **Dashboard:** Visual charts (Recharts) showing planned vs. delivered tonnage by region.
- **Printable Templates:**
    - **Bon de Livraison (BL):** Official delivery note with SOMA branding.
    - **Fin de Cession (FC):** Summary of all deliveries for a specific operator/phase.
    - **Feuille de Route (FR):** Driver instructions and route details.
- **Exports:** Ability to export data to Excel/CSV for external auditing.

### 5.6 Financial Tracking
- **Payments:** Track road fees, fuel costs, and loading/unloading expenses per delivery.
- **Admin Expenses:** Manage general operational costs categorized by type and analytical codes.

## 6. Technical Requirements
- **Frontend:** React 18, Vite, Tailwind CSS.
- **Backend:** Supabase (PostgreSQL) for data storage and real-time synchronization.
- **Icons:** Lucide React.
- **PDF Engine:** jsPDF with autoTable.
- **Date Handling:** Flatpickr for advanced range selection.

## 7. Database Schema Design
- `trucks`: Vehicle metadata and status.
- `drivers`: Personnel details and truck FK.
- `project`: Campaign phases and visibility flags.
- `allocations`: Target tonnage mapping.
- `deliveries`: Transactional logs for every shipment.
- `operators`: Recipient entities (Cooperatives/GIE).
- `payments`: Cost tracking linked to deliveries.
- `user_preferences`: Role-based access control.

## 8. Future Roadmap
- **Mobile App:** Dedicated PWA for drivers with offline scanning.
- **GPS Tracking:** Real-time truck location integration.
- **Automated Notifications:** SMS/Email alerts for operators upon truck departure.
